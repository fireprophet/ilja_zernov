Personal Consultations

I will conduct regular personal consultations for migrants where we can discuss any issues related to staying in Germany. This can include help with document preparation, legal advice, or simply a conversation about how to better integrate into German society.

Addressing Long Waiting Times for Citizenship

I understand how important it is for many migrants to obtain German citizenship. I will work to make this process more efficient and transparent. This includes dialogue with relevant authorities and finding solutions to optimize the procedure.

Support for Integration Projects

I will support and develop projects that help migrants better integrate into German society. This can include language learning assistance, cultural events, or professional orientation programs.

Expanding the Powers of the Migration Council

I will work to ensure that the Migration Council has more opportunities to influence decisions affecting migrants. This includes strengthening connections with city administration and other important institutions.
